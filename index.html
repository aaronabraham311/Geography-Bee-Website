What is Geography

Geography is not just the study of earth's physical processes. Rather, the study of geography includes economics, culture, languages and anything related to Earth. Through the geography club, you can understand more about the underlying factors of our political, cultural and physical worlds. By immersing in the world of geography, you can develop your understanding of:

spatial awareness
cultural appreciation
drivers of the world economy
